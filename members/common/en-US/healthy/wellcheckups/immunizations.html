Immunizations

Immunizations help keep your family healthy

Offered free to Molina Healthcare members, immunizations help protect you from getting sick.

Quick Tips:

  • Keep a record of your child's shots.
  • Bring the record with you to every provider's appointment so your provider knows what shots have been given and what shots are due.
  • Don't forget to ask your provider about the flu shot.
