Attention Job Seekers and Molina Applicants: Fraud Alert


It has been brought to our attention that third parties posing as Molina Healthcare have been soliciting money from job seekers. Molina has reported this activity to the appropriate law enforcement agencies for further investigation. To help you avoid becoming a victim of this fraud, we wanted to make you aware of this situation and let you know that Molina neither solicits money nor collects fees of any type from job applicants. If you feel you’ve been victimized, please report the scam to your local law enforcement authorities.

**Update: A website by the name of MolinaHealthcareInc.com has been communicating with applicants under false pretense. This website is not an official Molina Healthcare, Inc. website and you should ignore any communications associated with it.

Molina Healthcare is an equal opportunity employer and makes employment decisions on the basis of merit to ensure the best fit in every position.
